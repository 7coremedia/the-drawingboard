**Before rewriting a single line, you need to understand the five structural problems the current site has:**

**Problem 1 --- It Speaks to Nobody Specific**

The current site uses language like \'human-centered,\' \'visual sovereignty,\' \'institutional scaling\' --- language that sounds premium but tells a busy founder in London, a CMO in Toronto, or a startup founder in Atlanta absolutely nothing about what you actually do or what changes in their life after working with you. Premium language without specificity = beautiful confusion.

**Problem 2 --- Zero Proof of Outcome**

The Work section shows \'Project 01 --- Brand Identity\' repeated 18 times with no client names, no context, no story, no outcome. To an international client who has never heard of you, this is the visual equivalent of saying \'trust me.\' It creates zero conviction. Case studies that show transformation --- before and after, challenge and result --- convert clients. Portfolio tiles don\'t.

**Problem 3 --- The Hero Section Fails the 5-Second Test**

\'Crafting human brands and experiences since AI happened\' --- this is your first impression to every international client. It references AI in a way that anchors you to a trend rather than a truth. It says nothing about who you help, what you build, or what outcome they get. A visitor from outside Nigeria who doesn\'t know you has no reason to scroll down.

**Problem 4 --- The CTA is \'Apply\'**

The word \'Apply\' creates the feeling of an audition, which is fine for positioning scarcity --- but only if everything before it has already convinced them they want to be selected. Right now, nothing before it does that convincing. You have the gatekeeper energy without the authority signal that justifies it. The CTA architecture needs to be rebuilt from the ground up.

**Problem 5 --- Missing Sections That International Clients Require**

The current site is missing: (1) A clear About / Who We Are section with your story and POV, (2) Named, outcome-based case studies, (3) A specific Services page with pricing or pricing guidance in USD, (4) A direct contact mechanism beyond a form, (5) Trust signals --- testimonials, client names, results in numbers, (6) An SEO structure that lets international clients find you through search.
