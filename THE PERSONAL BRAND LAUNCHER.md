## 2. THE TRUST ARCHITECTURE: LEAP FRAMEWORK

### INTRODUCING: LEAP FRAMEWORK™

**L**ogic → **E**mpathy → **A**uthenticity → **P**urpose

**Why This Order Matters:**

1. **Logic** (credibility) = "Can I trust this person's thinking?"
2. **Empathy** (understanding) = "Do they understand me?"
3. **Authenticity** (realness) = "Are they being genuine?"
4. **Purpose** (mission) = "Why should I follow them?"

This sequence builds trust from foundation to inspiration.

---

### L - LOGIC: Credibility Through Clarity

**The Principle**: Clear thinking builds trust. Confused thinking destroys it.

**PROVIDE CLEAR REASONING:**

```
□ Can someone follow my thought process?
□ Do I explain the "why" behind my advice?
□ Are my frameworks simple to understand?
□ Do I use evidence, not just opinion?
□ Can I break complex ideas into clear steps?
```

**STRUCTURE YOUR THOUGHTS:**

```
□ Do I use frameworks and systems?
□ Is my content easy to scan and absorb?
□ Do I avoid jargon and insider language?
□ Can someone implement what I teach?
□ Do I provide specific examples?
```

# 1) AMPLIFY (Grounded in **AIDA + Relationship Loops**)

**Definition**  
AMPLIFY = a practical, audience-first application of AIDA (Awareness → Interest → Desire → Action) extended into a repeatable emotional loop for long-term advocacy.

**Core Components**

- **A — Awareness (AIDA):** Attention mechanisms (hooks, signal posts).
    
- **M — Mirror (Interest):** Reflect audience language/identity; use empathy phrases.
    
- **P — Personalize (Desire):** Micro-segmentation, tailored hooks/offers.
    
- **L — Listen (Action feedback):** Comments, DMs, micro-commitments (clicks, opt-ins).
    
- **I — Integrate (Retention):** Reframe input into content & product decisions.
    
- **F — Fortify (Proof):** Testimonials, stats, case studies.
    
- **Y — Yield (Advocacy):** Invite co-creation, UGC, refer-a-friend.
    

**Brand Application**

- Convert single posts into micro-funnels: hook → mirror → CTA → follow-up → social proof → advocacy.
    
- Use micro-commitments (e.g., reply “yes”) to move people from Interest → Desire.
    
- Run 30–60 day AMPLIFY loops (one emotional arc + one functional offer).
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Pick one hero emotion per campaign.
    
- Write 3 mirrors (audience phrases) to test.
    
- Create 1 micro-offer (low friction).
    
- Collect 5 pieces of proof within 60 days.
    
- Run one co-creation ask (poll, prompt) per month.

# 2) THE MONETIZATION FLYWHEEL (Built on **Value Ladder + Hook-Story-Offer + Subscription Funnel**)

**Definition**  
A cycle: attract → convert → deliver → retain → reinvest. It’s the Value Ladder (free → low → core → high), executed using Hook-Story-Offer (HSO) creative and subscription/continuity models to compound revenue.

**Core Components**

- **Value Magnet (Top):** Free content + lead magnet (educational/entertaining).
    
- **Entry Offer:** Low-ticket, high-value (workshop, mini-course).
    
- **Core Offer:** Signature program or product (transformational).
    
- **Ascension:** High-ticket coaching, mastermind, agency retainers.
    
- **Continuity:** Subscription/membership + community.
    
- **Proof & Optimization:** Testimonials, conversion metrics, payment data.
    
- **Reinvestment:** Spend on content + creators that accelerate flywheel.
    

**Brand Application**

- Use **HSO** in funnels: Hook (one-sentence attention), Story (empathy + result), Offer (clear outcome + scarcity/guarantee).
    
- Map content to where prospects sit on your Value Ladder; tailor CTAs per level.
    
- Convert buyers to community (continuity) for recurring revenue and advocacy.
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Define clear outcome for each rung of your Value Ladder.
    
- Create 1 Hook, 1 Story, 1 Offer template and reuse.
    
- Build a 3-email sequence for entry→core conversion.
    
- Launch a continuity product once you hit 100 core customers.

# 3) DISTRIBUTION PRISM (Based on **PESO + 70/20/10 + Repurposing Matrix**)

**Definition**  
A systematic approach to channel selection and reuse: Paid, Earned, Shared, Owned (PESO), plus a content split and repurposing matrix so a single idea becomes many assets.

**Core Components**

- **PESO:** Paid ads; Earned press/mentions; Shared (social/influencers); Owned (newsletter, podcast).
    
- **70/20/10 Content Rule:** 70% core pillars (evergreen), 20% experimental, 10% trend/viral.
    
- **Repurposing Matrix:** One long-form asset → X short formats (long post → thread → 3 reels → carousel → newsletter).
    
- **Platform Fit:** Match message style to platform psychology (LinkedIn = professional logic, TikTok = quick patterns, YouTube = teach/long-form).
    

**Brand Application**

- Each core message (your “diamond”) is refracted into PESO outputs by priority.
    
- Use the 70/20/10 split for risk management and algorithm experiments.
    
- Track platform ROI (time invested vs. results) and adjust.
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- For each monthly theme: create 1 long-form asset + repurpose into 5 micro-assets.
    
- Allocate budget: 60% Owned growth, 30% Paid experiments, 10% Earned/outreach.
    
- Log time spent vs. engagement to calculate platform efficiency.

# 4) RESONANCE LADDER (Adaptation of **See-Think-Do-Care / AIDA**)

**Definition**  
A five-stage map that shows how people move from passive viewers to identity-aligned advocates.

**Levels**

1. **Awareness (See)** — Recognize you exist.
    
2. **Interest (Think)** — Consume multiple assets.
    
3. **Trust (Do - Micro-commit)** — Micro-actions (opt-ins, replies).
    
4. **Affinity (Buy/Engage)** — Purchases + emotional connection.
    
5. **Identity Fusion (Care)** — They adopt your beliefs; refer others.
    

**Brand Application**

- Assign KPI per level (reach, average session, opt-ins, conversions, NPS/referral rate).
    
- Create content ladders that move people one step at a time (awareness → education → social proof → offer).
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Audit 5 recent posts: which ladder step do they serve?
    
- Create a 3-step nurture for Trust → Affinity: email + social proof + limited offer.
    
- Measure conversion uplift after adding proof elements.

# 5) MAGNETIC TRUST EQUATION (Rooted in **Maister’s Trust Equation + Cialdini**)

**Definition / Formula**  
**Trust ≈ (Credibility + Reliability + Intimacy) / Self-Orientation** (Maister)  
Applied: **(Authority + Consistency + Warmth) + Social Proof — Self-Focus = Loyalty**

**Core Components**

- **Credibility / Authority:** Expertise & evidence (credentials, case data).
    
- **Reliability / Consistency:** Predictability of behavior and content.
    
- **Intimacy / Warmth:** Empathy, vulnerability, private responses.
    
- **Lower Self-Orientation:** Audience-first language; less sell-first posture.
    
- **Cialdini Triggers:** Social proof, authority, scarcity, liking, reciprocity, consistency.
    

**Brand Application**

- Use credibility markers in logic-heavy content; use intimacy in community spaces; reduce self-orientation in public posts.
    
- Add explicit proof blocks to offers (before/after, testimonials, numbers).
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Add one credibility item to every pillar piece (data, study, or client story).
    
- Post consistently on 2 platforms for 90 days to build reliability.
    
- Respond personally to 10 DMs/week to build intimacy.

# 6) DEEP STORY MODEL (Anchored in **Hero’s Journey + Dan Harmon’s Story Circle**)

**Definition**  
A reusable story architecture for origin, transformation, and offer narratives that balance vulnerability and authority.

**Core Stages**

1. **Home (Context):** Who you were/where you started.
    
2. **Need / Call (Want):** The desire or problem.
    
3. **Crossing / Failure (Obstacle):** The conflict or low point.
    
4. **Mentor / Method (Turning):** The discovery or framework.
    
5. **Trial (Proof):** Tests, failures, small wins.
    
6. **Transformation (Result):** Proof of change.
    
7. **Return (Embodiment):** How life is now and the new norm.
    
8. **Transfer (Call to Action):** How the audience can replicate it (offer).
    

**Brand Application**

- For sales pages: compress stages 1→3 into empathetic lead; 4→6 as method + proof; 7→8 as the clear CTA + guarantee.
    
- For content: rotate story types (origin, troubleshooting, meta-reflections) to avoid story fatigue.
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Draft 3 brand stories using all 8 stages (short, medium, long formats).
    
- Identify the “Flaw” and keep it visible as your human anchor.
    
- Use one transformation story each month in email + social + landing page.

# 7) PSYCHOLOGY OF CONVERSION (Combines **Fogg Behavior Model + Micro-Commitments + Cognitive Biases**)

**Definition**  
Conversion is behavior: it needs Motivation + Ability + Prompt (Fogg). Use micro-commitments and cognitive nudges to create predictable actions.

**Core Components**

- **Motivation:** Emotional drivers (pain → avoidance, aspiration → approach).
    
- **Ability:** Reduce friction; make actions simple.
    
- **Prompt:** Strong CTA at the right moment.
    
- **Micro-Commitments:** Small asks that escalate (like → comment → join → buy).
    
- **Biases/Nudges:** Loss aversion, social proof, authority, scarcity, framing.
    

**Brand Application**

- Structure funnels that start with a tiny ask (reply/comment) and escalate.
    
- Reduce ability friction (one-click leads, auto-fill, simple checkout).
    
- Use social proof adjacent to CTAs (quote, logo bar, quantified result).
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Map your funnel with Motivation/Ability/Prompt for each step.
    
- Create 3 micro-commit actions in pre-sale content.
    
- Add a trust cue and a friction-reducer to each landing page.

# 8) CONTENT MULTIPLICATION SYSTEM (Practical Repurposing Template)

**Definition**  
A disciplined matrix that turns 1 flagship idea into 6–12 assets across platforms.

**Core Steps**

2. **Core Extracts:** Key quotes, frameworks, and moments.
    
3. **Micro Assets:** 3–5 short clips, tweets/threads, 1 carousel, 1 email sequence.

**Brand Application**

- Publish flagship on Owned first (newsletter, YouTube), then feed micro assets into social and paid.
    

**Tactical Checklist**

- Create a repurposing template: 1 flagship → 5 clips → 1 thread → 1 carousel → 1 newsletter.
    
- Schedule assets to go live over 10 days (not all at once).
    
- Tag performance per format to decide future 70/20/10 allocations.
