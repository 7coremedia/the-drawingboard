Phase III

Phase III: The LEAP Framework

People don't buy what you do, they buy why you do it—and whether they trust you. Trust is architected through the LEAP sequence.

(L) Logic

Credibility through clarity.
Can someone follow your thought process? Do you use frameworks? Confusion destroys trust immediately.

(E) Empathy

Understanding creates connection.
Do you reflect their pain accurately? Use "You" more than "I"? Meet them exactly where they are?

(A) Authenticity

Genuine presence.
Embrace imperfection. Share learned lessons, not unprocessed trauma. Your flaws make your expertise believable.

(P) Purpose

Mission beyond self.
Why should they follow you? What change are you driving? Purpose converts followers into believers.
